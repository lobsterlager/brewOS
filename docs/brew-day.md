# Brew Day Walkthrough

How Batch #001 was brewed on February 21, 2026.

## Setup (07:30–08:15)

1. Grainfather G30 positioned on balcony
2. Water filled to 24.5 L mark
3. brewOS launched on MacBook
4. BLE connection established to G30
5. Recipe loaded from `recipes/batch-001.yaml`
6. Vision camera mounted and calibrated
7. Clawdbot (Telegram) active for approvals

## Mash (08:15–10:15)

OpenClaw managed the full mash schedule:

| Step | Temp | Duration | Notes |
|------|------|----------|-------|
| Preheat | 65°C | — | Water heating, no grain |
| Infusion | 64°C | — | Grain in (5.22 kg) |
| Maltoserast | 63°C | 35 min | Beta-amylase rest |
| Saccharification | 72°C | 30 min | Alpha-amylase rest |
| Mashout | 78°C | 10 min | Denature enzymes |

Each transition requested via Clawdbot, approved by Gerhard.

## Boil (10:20–11:50)

- 90-minute boil at 100°C
- Hop additions managed by timer alerts via Clawdbot
- Vision system monitored foam levels continuously
- All four hop additions confirmed and logged

## Chill & Transfer (11:50–12:15)

- Wort chiller deployed
- Cooled to 12°C pitch temperature
- Transferred to conical fermenter
- Two packs W-34/70 pitched
- Glycol chiller set to maintain 12°C

## Result

24.5 L of amber wort at 12.2°P, fermenting at 12°C. Session complete at 12:15.
